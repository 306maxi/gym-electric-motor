Motor Dashboard¶

class gym_electric_motor.visualization.motor_dashboard.MotorDashboard(update_period=0.05, visu_period=5, plotted_variables='all', **_)[source]¶

Visualization of the variables of the motor in graphs. This can be the angular velocity omega, torque phase voltages, phase currents, supply voltage and the rotor angle at the PMSM.

Constructor of the dashboard.

Parameters

  • plotted_variables – Names of the variables that shall be shown on the dashboard | Shortcut: [‘all’]/[‘none’] for all/no visualized variables

  • update_period – Number of seconds after that dashboard will be updated | Updating with tiny periods lead to very low speed.

  • visu_period – Time period shown on the dashboard
